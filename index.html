Family Story Tree

  • Our startup is building a Digital Family Story Tree for preserving family memories.
  • The application lets each family member add stories, photos, recipes, and advice in one shared space.
  • Memories grow visually like a tree, making it easy and engaging to explore family history across generations.
  • The goal is to prevent traditions and personal stories from being lost or scattered across devices and apps.
  • We focus on emotional connection, collaborative storytelling, and long-term preservation of family heritage.
